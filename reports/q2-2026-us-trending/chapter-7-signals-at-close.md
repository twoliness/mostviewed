## Methodology

- **Chart-holders sample:** `video_summary` rows where `last_seen ≥ 2026-06-24` AND `days_on_chart ≥ 3` AND `peak_rank ≤ 25`. Sorted by `days_on_chart DESC, peak_rank ASC`, top 15 shown.
- **Breakout candidates sample:** `breakout_candidates` rows where `detected_at ≥ 2026-06-25`. Ordered by score DESC. The breakout-detection layer was deployed 2026-06-29 — the row selection window is 2 days of production data. Every candidate in the underlying data during that window is shown; nothing filtered.
- **Score definition:** composite of velocity at detection, age at detection, and source-chart depth. Higher score = stronger initial breakout signal. Score does not predict eventual chart position, only breakout intensity.
- **Confirmed vs. killed:** `breakout_candidates.status` field. Set by the refresh-breakouts cron running every 30 minutes. `confirmed` = candidate maintained above sustained-velocity threshold after refresh. `killed` = candidate dropped below sustained threshold within 24 hours of detection.
- **Sample size caveat:** with only 2 days of coverage, base rate estimates for the breakout-candidate population are noisy. A Q3 report will have a full quarter of detector data to draw from — those base rates will be publishable.
